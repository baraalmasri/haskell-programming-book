For each expression below, determine whether it's - 
1. `Normal form`, which implies `weak head normal form`;
2. `Weak head normal form` only; or
3. neither.

1. `[1,2,3,4,5]`

**Ans** Normal form

2. `1 : 2 : 3 : 4 : _`

**Ans** Weak normal form

3. `enumFromTo 1 10`

**Ans** Neither

4. `length [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]`

**Ans** Neither

5. `sum (enumFromTo 1 10)`

**Ans** Neither

6. `['a'..'m'] ++ ['n'..'z']`

**Ans** Neither


7. `(_, 'b')`

**Ans** Weak normal form
